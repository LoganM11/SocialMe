Instagram

First Login Guide

  • After account creation, click the Next button repeatedly until you reach the home screen. This step is crucial to avoid video uploading errors.

Sign-Up Information Guide

  • Use a valid email address or phone number to create your account.
  • Ensure your password includes a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Double-check your username availability before finalizing registration.

Video Requirements Guide

  • Maximum video length: 60 seconds for posts and up to 15 minutes for IGTV.
  • Supported file format: MP4.
  • Aspect ratio: 1:1 (square) or 9:16 (vertical).

Debug Error Guide

  • Issue: Video upload errors. Solution: Ensure all Next buttons are clicked during sign-up and your app is updated.
  • Issue: Video fails to auto-cut. Solution: Re-export your video in the correct aspect ratio before uploading.

Snapchat

First Login Guide

  • You must sign in on a mobile device to enable video uploads. While emulators are an option, they are not recommended.

Sign-Up Information Guide

  • Create your account using a valid email or phone number.
  • Verify your phone number to unlock all features.

Video Requirements Guide

  • Maximum video length: 60 seconds (split into 10-second segments).
  • Supported file format: MP4.
  • Aspect ratio: 9:16 (vertical).

Debug Error Guide

  • Issue: Videos fail to upload. Solution: Ensure you're signed in through the official mobile app and check your internet connection.
  • Issue: Emulators causing errors. Solution: Switch to a physical device for better compatibility.

YouTube

First Login Guide

  • Login credentials will be tied to your Google account.
  • Cookies will generate default uploading behavior, which can be customized later in YouTube's settings.

Sign-Up Information Guide

  • Use a Google account to access YouTube. If you don’t have one, create a Google account first.
  • Set a strong password and enable two-factor authentication for added security.

Video Requirements Guide

  • Maximum video length: 12 hours or 128GB.
  • Supported file formats: MP4, MOV, AVI, etc.
  • Aspect ratio: 16:9 (landscape).

Debug Error Guide

  • Issue: Videos upload as long-form instead of shorts. Solution: Ensure your video is under 60 seconds and set to the correct aspect ratio (vertical).
  • Issue: Uploading halts midway. Solution: Check your internet connection and ensure the file size doesn’t exceed 128GB.
